Makoto Shinkai was born on February 9, 1973. He is from Koumi, Japan, and is currently a Japanese film director, animator, author, and artist.

After graduating Chuo University in 1996, he went to work at Falcom, a video game company. He did animation work and graphic design there for 5 years.

In 1999, he released his first piece of original video animation, "She and Her Cat." The five-minute short film was done entirely done with Adobe After Effects and his own hand-drawn pictures.

His first work received multiple awards such as the grand prize at the 12th DoGA CG Animation contest in the year 2000, which led to him entering his association with CoMix Wave Films.

Ever since then, he has made many more films that have received incredible public acclaim, and even topped Spirited Away as the highest grossing anime film worldwide with the release of "your name." in 2016.
