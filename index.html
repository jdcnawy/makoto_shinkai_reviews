This website is all about Makoto Shinkai!

Ever since I came across his films around three years ago, I've been in love with his directing style.

At the top of the page, you'll see a navigation bar that will take you to different sections of the site. This is the homepage.

Then, we have a page giving a little bit of insight into Makoto Shinkai himself.

There will also be a link to his website for those of you that are interested!

Keep in mind that all reviews on this site are my opinion only! Don't take anything I say here as gospel.

The far-right "Movies" tab is a dropdown menu that links to my personal reviews of all his films! (I excluded his projects that were shorter than 20 minutes, because there wasn't much to say.)

There will be no major spoilers, but I will talk about the events that take place in the first sections of each film for the sake of setting the scene.
